Eighteen practice questions across the four domains. Try them **timed** (about 90 seconds each), then check the **Answer Key** at the bottom. Concepts are in the **SAT Math Knowledge Points** note.

---

## A. Algebra

**1.** If $5x - 3 = 2x + 9$, what is $x$?

**2.** A line has slope $-2$ and passes through $(1, 4)$. What is its $y$-intercept?

**3.** For what value of $b$ do $y = 3x + b$ and $y = 3x - 5$ have no solution as a system?

**4.** If $\dfrac{x}{4} + 2 = 7$, what is $x$?

**5.** A gym charges a \$30 join fee plus \$20 per month. Which equation gives total cost $C$ after $m$ months?

## B. Advanced Math

**6.** Solve $x^2 + 2x - 15 = 0$.

**7.** The parabola $y = x^2 - 8x + 12$ has its vertex at what $(x, y)$?

**8.** If $9^{x} = 27$, what is $x$?

**9.** Factor completely: $4x^2 - 25$.

**10.** A population doubles every year and starts at 500. Write a function $P(t)$ for the population after $t$ years.

## C. Problem-Solving & Data Analysis

**11.** A \$120 item is marked up 15%. What is the new price?

**12.** A recipe needs 3 cups of flour for 12 cookies. How many cups for 30 cookies?

**13.** The data set is $5, 8, 8, 9, 10, 14$. Find the mean and median.

**14.** A bag has 4 red, 6 blue, and 10 green marbles. What is the probability of drawing a blue marble?

**15.** A car uses 8 gallons to travel 256 miles. What is the fuel efficiency in miles per gallon?

## D. Geometry & Trigonometry

**16.** A right triangle has legs 9 and 12. Find the hypotenuse.

**17.** A circle has circumference $10\pi$. What is its area?

**18.** In a right triangle, $\theta$ has adjacent side 8 and hypotenuse 17. Find $\tan\theta$.

---

## Answer Key

1. **$x = 4$** — $3x = 12$.
2. **$6$** — $4 = -2(1) + b \Rightarrow b = 6$.
3. **$b \ne -5$** (any value except $-5$) — parallel lines with different intercepts; e.g. $b = 2$.
4. **$x = 20$** — $\frac{x}{4} = 5$.
5. **$C = 20m + 30$**.
6. **$x = 3$ or $x = -5$** — $(x+5)(x-3)=0$.
7. **$(4, -4)$** — $x=-\frac{-8}{2}=4$, $y=16-32+12=-4$.
8. **$x = \tfrac{3}{2}$** — $9^x=3^{2x}=3^3 \Rightarrow 2x=3$.
9. **$(2x+5)(2x-5)$** — difference of squares.
10. **$P(t) = 500\cdot 2^{t}$**.
11. **\$138** — $120\times1.15$.
12. **$7.5$ cups** — $\frac{3}{12}=\frac{x}{30}$.
13. **Mean $9$, median $8.5$** — sum $54/6=9$; middle two $8,9 \Rightarrow 8.5$.
14. **$\tfrac{6}{20}=\tfrac{3}{10}=0.3$**.
15. **$32$ mpg** — $256/8$.
16. **$15$** — $\sqrt{81+144}=\sqrt{225}$.
17. **$25\pi$** — $2\pi r=10\pi \Rightarrow r=5$; area $\pi(5)^2$.
18. **$\tfrac{15}{8}$** — opposite $=\sqrt{17^2-8^2}=15$; $\tan=\frac{opp}{adj}=\frac{15}{8}$.
