## Algebra

- ★ Slope-intercept: $y = mx + b$. Point-slope: $y - y_1 = m(x - x_1)$. Standard: $Ax + By = C$.
- ★ Slope: $m = \dfrac{y_2-y_1}{x_2-x_1}$. Parallel ⇒ equal $m$; perpendicular ⇒ $m_1 m_2 = -1$.
- ★ Systems: one solution (different slopes), none (parallel, $\ne$ intercept), infinite (identical).
- ★ Linear function from a table: constant rate of change between rows.
- Absolute value: $|x| = a \Rightarrow x = \pm a$.

## Advanced Math

- ★ Quadratic formula: $x = \dfrac{-b\pm\sqrt{b^2-4ac}}{2a}$. Discriminant $b^2-4ac$ → 2 / 1 / 0 real roots.
- ★ Forms of a parabola: standard $ax^2+bx+c$; vertex $a(x-h)^2+k$ (vertex $(h,k)$); factored $a(x-r_1)(x-r_2)$ (roots $r_1,r_2$).
- ★ Axis/vertex: $x = -\dfrac{b}{2a}$. Sum of roots $= -\dfrac{b}{a}$, product $= \dfrac{c}{a}$.
- ★ Exponents: $x^a x^b = x^{a+b}$; $(x^a)^b=x^{ab}$; $x^{-a}=\frac1{x^a}$; $x^{m/n}=\sqrt[n]{x^m}$.
- ★ Difference of squares: $a^2-b^2=(a+b)(a-b)$. Perfect square: $a^2\pm2ab+b^2=(a\pm b)^2$.
- ★ Exponential growth/decay: $y = a(1\pm r)^t$ (rate $r$ per period).

## Problem-Solving & Data Analysis

- ★ Percent change: $\dfrac{\text{new}-\text{old}}{\text{old}}\times100\%$. Increase ×$(1+r)$, decrease ×$(1-r)$.
- ★ Proportions: set up equal ratios; unit rate = quantity per 1 unit.
- ★ Mean $=\frac{\text{sum}}{n}$; median = middle; mode = most frequent; range = max−min.
- ★ More spread ⇒ larger standard deviation. Outliers pull the **mean**, not the median.
- ★ Probability $=\dfrac{\text{favorable}}{\text{total}}$; expected value uses probabilities × values.
- ★ Two-way tables: conditional probability uses the relevant row/column total.
- ★ Larger random sample ⇒ smaller margin of error.

## Geometry & Trigonometry

- Circle: area $\pi r^2$, circumference $2\pi r$. Arc length $=\frac{\theta}{360}\,2\pi r$; sector area $=\frac{\theta}{360}\pi r^2$.
- ★ Equation of circle: $(x-h)^2+(y-k)^2=r^2$.
- Triangle area $\frac12 bh$; rectangle $bh$; the reference sheet gives volumes (prism, cylinder, sphere, cone, pyramid).
- Pythagorean: $a^2+b^2=c^2$. ★ Triples: 3-4-5, 5-12-13, 8-15-17.
- Special triangles: $45\text{-}45\text{-}90 \to x,x,x\sqrt2$; $\;30\text{-}60\text{-}90 \to x,x\sqrt3,2x$.
- ★ SOH-CAH-TOA: $\sin=\frac{opp}{hyp},\cos=\frac{adj}{hyp},\tan=\frac{opp}{adj}$. ★ $\sin\theta=\cos(90^\circ-\theta)$.
- ★ Similar figures: angles equal, sides proportional; areas scale by $k^2$, volumes by $k^3$.
- Angles: straight line $180^\circ$; triangle $180^\circ$; vertical angles equal; parallel lines cut by a transversal → equal corresponding/alternate angles.
