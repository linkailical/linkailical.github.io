机考 SAT 考的是**语境词义**，不是生僻词死记。下面是常见、偏学术语气、且**语义细微差别重要**的词。每个给中文释义 + 英文例句。建议先遮住释义、按句意猜。

> 单词与例句保留英文（SAT 为英文考试），释义用中文。

---

## 论证与观点类

- **substantiate** (v.) 用证据支持，证实。*The study substantiates the claim that sleep aids memory.*
- **undermine** (v.) 削弱。*New data undermine the older theory.*
- **nuanced** (adj.) 有细微差别的，细致的。*Her nuanced argument avoided simple labels.*
- **tenuous** (adj.) 薄弱的，站不住脚的。*The link between the two events is tenuous.*
- **compelling** (adj.) 有说服力的，引人信服的。*The evidence is compelling.*
- **speculative** (adj.) 推测性的（无确证）。*His conclusion is speculative.*
- **empirical** (adj.) 基于观察/实验的，实证的。*Empirical data support the model.*
- **refute** (v.) 驳倒，证伪。*The experiment refutes the hypothesis.*

## 语气与态度类

- **measured** (adj.) 审慎克制的。*A measured response, neither angry nor dismissive.*
- **ambivalent** (adj.) 矛盾的，喜忧参半的。*She is ambivalent about the change.*
- **skeptical** (adj.) 怀疑的。*Readers remained skeptical of the bold claim.*
- **candid** (adj.) 坦率直接的。*A candid admission of error.*
- **indifferent** (adj.) 漠不关心的。*He was indifferent to the outcome.*
- **earnest** (adj.) 真诚认真的。*An earnest plea for support.*

## 变化与过程类

- **mitigate** (v.) 减轻，缓解。*Planting trees can mitigate flooding.*
- **exacerbate** (v.) 加剧，使恶化。*The delay exacerbated the shortage.*
- **proliferate** (v.) 激增。*Apps proliferated after the launch.*
- **diminish** (v.) 减少。*Interest diminished over time.*
- **catalyst** (n.) 催化剂；促因。*The report was a catalyst for reform.*
- **transient** (adj.) 短暂的。*A transient improvement.*

## 通用学术词

- **plausible** (adj.) 看似可信的。*A plausible explanation.*
- **arbitrary** (adj.) 任意的，无依据的。*An arbitrary rule.*
- **autonomous** (adj.) 自主的，自治的。*An autonomous region.*
- **paradox** (n.) 悖论，似非而是。*The paradox of choice.*
- **scrutiny** (n.) 仔细审查。*The plan faced scrutiny.*
- **prevalent** (adj.) 普遍的。*The view is prevalent among experts.*
- **viable** (adj.) 可行的。*A viable alternative.*
- **discern** (v.) 觉察，分辨。*Hard to discern a pattern.*
- **novel** (adj.) 新颖的（也可作名词=小说）。*A novel approach.*
- **redundant** (adj.) 多余重复的。*Cut the redundant phrase.*

## 自测（遮住上面的词）

1. 使问题恶化 = ______
2. 看似可信但未证实 = ______
3. 用证据支持 = ______
4. 短暂的 = ______
5. 喜忧参半的 = ______

> **答案：** 1. exacerbate 2. plausible（或 speculative）3. substantiate 4. transient 5. ambivalent。

**学习建议：** 练习中遇到生词，别只记中文翻译——记下它出现的**句子**。SAT 词义永远由语境决定。
